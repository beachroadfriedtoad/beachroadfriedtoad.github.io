20251115 • Garden Update

Here is where the garden is at half–way through November…



Spinach grew from direct sow. Spring onions to the left are very slow but haven’t died. 2 Apollo tomatoes and a yellow one on the far left.

Finally built a better frame for the netting on the pallet bed.




Planted my cucumber, wombok, cos and basil seedlings I’ve grown from seed. Also planted 3 strawberry plants I had, wasn’t really sure what to do with them so planted them in the end bed.
