Treats of Tasmania

A post dedicated to the treats of Tassie. Will add more as I find them.

1. Scottsdale Cottage Bakery
Ask for an egg roll – warmed in the microwave with butter and relish. $3.50. Chef’s kiss.



2. Chilla’s Takeaway Devonport
Breakfast wrap. Egg, bacon, hashbrown, cheese, BBQ sauce.



3. Latrobe Bakery
Egg & bacon roll. $2.80 each.



4. Detention River Store
Sweet Chilli, Cheese and Bacon, Chicken Schnitzel Roll. $10.80. Crazy invention.
